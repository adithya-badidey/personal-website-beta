Humans, Cultural Evolution and Resilience

Picking up from Natural Selection, Orchids and humans

Humans have existed in their current form for around 100,000 years. In this time, we have domesticated plants and animals and becomes more settled - building houses, and creating a concept of ownership. And life has been more or less comfortable. We havn't had to struggle against any other species in a big way. All the excess energy we have had as a species has been used to imprint ourselves in this world, to make ourselves more comfortable, to create structures with which to manage ourselves.
