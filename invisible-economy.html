Invisible Economy

What is the Invisible Economy?

A radical seperation of the making from the market using Transparency and Invisibility as core mechanisms. Although intended for artists, this idea is applicable to any kind of making-, code writing, curating, journalling, etc.

Transparency and Invisibility

There are two main mechanisms involved:

  • Invisibility means that the mechanisms of value exchange are invisble to the artist. What this is intended to do is to distance the artist the extrinsic motivations and bring them closer to their intrinsic ones.
    • This means that price-setting, negotiations, etc will be invisible to the artist.
  • Transparency by using blockchain for all transactions ensures that the system is fair and open. This is indended to create confidence in the minds of the artists so they can focus on their art

It means:

    • Artists wont be able to decide the price of their art
    • Purchasers will not be able to see how much others are buying for
