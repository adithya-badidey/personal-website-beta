How to build a Zettelkasten using Tiddlywiki

I have found tiddlywiki to be an effective tool to build a Zettelkasten. I will try to describe some of my thoughts behind it here.

There are three types of notes

  • Fleeting notes: These notes also those not captured on the TiddlyWiki. They are captured elsewhere with the intention of converting into a permanent note.
  • Bibliographic notes : I use the source tag for this
  • Permanent notes: These form the bulk of the Zettelkasten

How to take notes

  • When writing notes, start from the Source.
    • Make a note tagged source, enter the details of the source.
    • Each idea from this source should be linked from here.
  • Capture each idea as a seperate note in your TiddlyWiki.
  • Title with the least words with which you describe the idea
  • Add the appropriate tags
  • First paragraph of the note should summarize the idea.
  • Write in full sentences and with good grammer.
    • Your notes should be as short and simple as possible and no shorter.
    • Notes in a topic new to you will be longer - its fine.
    • You are not writing this for yourself, but for your future self.
  • After you insert a note, add more notes to connect that note to other notes.
    • How does this new idea connect, support, contradict, correct or add to other notes?
    • Backlinks plugin handles the work of adding references for us.
    • Think of the contexts under which you might want to stumble across your new idea.
  • Read more to fill in the gaps and develop your arguments
    • Be open to change.
  • Build and alter the system to suit your needs - A trusted system sets you free
