Nature and Sustainability

  • Nature has different meanings for different people and different viewpoints.
    • This understanding changes often
    • Common understandings need to be negotiated and contested by different groups holding different world views
    • Tribals might view the Niyamgiri as a sacred mountain whereas Industrialists see it as a source of cheap bauxite and Conservationists see it as a pristine jungle
  • Industrial societies often see nature as just a resource to be exploited
    • This is a reductionist view
    • No heed is given to other viewpoints and meanings of nature
    • This is one of the problems with Capitalism
