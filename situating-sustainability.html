Situating Sustainability

The movement of societies is determined by various factors. Some of the important ones are Nature, Culture, Markets and State. We try to understand each of them below:

  • Nature and Sustainability
    • Nature is the world all around us.
  • Culture and Sustainability
    • Culture is the world of meanings in which we live.
  • Markets and Sustainability
    • Markets have always existed
    • Historically, markets were embedded in society. Today, society is embedded in markets - markets define how our society is organized.
  • State and Sustainability
    • State (which is any government or organization with monopoly in political power) hold immense power in discussions about sustainability.

The important part are that all these are in a state of change and proper measures can lead us to a better outcome.
