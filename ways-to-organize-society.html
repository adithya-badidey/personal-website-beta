Ways to organize society

The primary ethical emperative for organizing a society is that its members’ wellbeing be maximised and their suffering minimised, within the context of a responsibly managed environment

There are several ways to organize a society:

  • Using Market or Market Economy
    • What that means is that allowing people to exchange goods and services in a true competitive, open and free market is the best way to have an equitable and just society. Marketplace design is essential to achieve this goal.
  • Moral Economy
  • Using the Caste System
  • Family System
