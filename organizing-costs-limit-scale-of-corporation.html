Organizing costs limit scale of Corporation

In a corporation, the manager directs the production. A sort of Central Planning which has the same problems as the Flaws of Central Planning

There are two factors reasons for the firm have higher organization costs and increased managerial mistages as it grows larger:

  • It becomes more spatially spread out (this has been highly reduced by telecommunications and the Internet)
  • It deals with more varied types of transactions
  • Corporations will get bigger:
    • the less the costs of organizing and the slower these costs rise with an increase in the transactions organized.
    • the less likely the entrepreneur is to make mistakes and the smaller the increase in mistakes with an increase in the transactions organized.
    • the greater the lowering (or the less the rise) in the supply price of factors of production to firms of larger size.
