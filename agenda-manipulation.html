Agenda Manipulation

Agenda Manipulation is the process where, by reframing the agenda and ordering decisions in the right way, any decision can be reached in a Democracy.

This desired result is achieved by taking advantage of Majority Cycling: making sure that at each stage of decision making, a different composition of majority determines the outcome. The authority taking decisions about the decision making process needs to know the preference of the participants to ensure the desired outcome by shifting majorities.
