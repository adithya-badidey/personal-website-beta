Social Media

Social spaces are like a projection of the real world into a virtual space. Each online persona is a projection of a real person.

Each expression of the online persona comes from their private state. That being said, private state is not truly private - each person's private state could be seen as a product of their previous state, the state of the world and the expressions of those connected to the person.

An independent expression can be seen as an statement of this private state, whereas a reactive expression can be seen as a response of this private state to the incoming communication. This reactive expression will be positive in case the incoming communication reinforces or coexists with their private state and will be negative if the incoming communication is opposing their private state.

By doing this, participants of the social space are able to use meaningful interactions to negotiate their reality in the shared social space.
