Proof of Personhood

How to create a proof of personhood or proof of humanity without another facebook or CPC?

Factors to keep in mind

  • Human verification
    • AI can create artificial faces/voices/photos that look real so humans cannot validate identities trivially
  • Web of trust
    • Leads to nodes with more centrality than others
  • One dimensional identity is not enough
    • Easier to subvert
    • Dangerous in the wrong hands
    • Multidimensionality of identity is required
  • Intersectional Identity
    • Using social intersections and belonging to create an identity

Evaluate the right to use the technology, not the identity - Edward Snowden

Examples of some efforts

  • Kleros: uses jurors who judge based on video evidence
  • Idena Network: uses Turing test which are released synchronously to test personhood

  • BrightId
