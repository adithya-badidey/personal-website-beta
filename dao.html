DAO

aka Distributed Autonomous Organization

Why DAOs?

  • Mistakes by managers is one of the limiting costs of traditional Corporations.
  • Flaws of Central Planning effect large corporations too
  • Cannot leverage the wisdom of the crowd - the people who really understand the ground reality
  • Lots of beaurocratic overhead is another limiting costs of traditional corporation

What is a company?

  • Contacts/Bylaws
  • Employees and other objects interact with these contracts and bylaws
  • Enforcement of bylaws
  • Manage money

Features

  • Runs only as code
  • Decentalized
  • Autonomous
  • Hosted on the Ethereum blockchain
  • Uses Smart Contract
