Transaction costs of the market

There are many transaction costs in a market:

  • Price Discovery - Bargaining with the party or paying an information broker
  • Taxes like sales tax - by the government
  • Cost of policing and enforcement of contracts
  • Quotas or price controls
  • Search and Information costs
  • Cost of keeping trade secrets
