Seeds

Seeds are saved usually in families? Gayatri from Sahaja Saguvali: * Ukkadagatri, Harihara, Near Tungabhadra - Karibasaveshwara? - During jatra Farmers from X community bring the seeds and throw at the ratha, distributing to all and exchange seeds. People who attend the ceremony take back the seeds multiply them and bring them back later.

  • Seed banks were there at individual family level, as well as community level.
