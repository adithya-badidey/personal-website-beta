Sustainability Action and You

These are notes from "Module 6: Sustainability action and you" of APU's Online Course - Sustainability in the Indian Context

  • Sustainability is a journey of change and not a destination
  • There is no recipe for sustainability. Sustainability is completely dependant on context and environment
  • Sustainable action needs to be nimble, flexible and adaptable
  • Sustainable action needs to be on different scales - Temporal, Geographical and Administrative
  • Action can be taken on individual, local collective, market, state or global levels
    • Many ecological problems can be across administrative boundaries so its important to have collective state action.
